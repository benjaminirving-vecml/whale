Baleen Whales

Baleen whales have baleen plates instead of teeth, which they use to filter small organisms like krill and plankton from seawater.

  • Blue Whale: The largest animal on Earth, growing up to 100 feet long.
  • Humpback Whale: Known for spectacular breaching and complex songs.
  • Gray Whale: Famous for its long migrations along coasts.

Toothed Whales

Toothed whales have teeth and use echolocation to hunt fish, squid, and other marine animals.

  • Sperm Whale: The largest toothed predator, diving deep for squid.
  • Orca (Killer Whale): Highly social and intelligent, apex predators.
  • Beluga: Known as the "canary of the sea" for their vocalizations.
